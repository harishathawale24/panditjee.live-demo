Namkaran Puja

"Hindu naming ceremony, 'Namkaran,' signifies religious, social, and legal importance. Held around the twelfth day post-birth, it strengthens familial bonds, introduces faith, and emphasizes parental responsibilities. Namkaran Sanskar Puja shapes the child's character per Vedic guidelines."

Griha Pravesh Puja

"Griha Pravesh Puja, the house inauguration ceremony, occurs before a family enters their new home. A priest selects an auspicious time using astrology. Approva, Sapoorva, and Dwandwah Griha Pravesh are distinct rituals, while online pandits offer convenient services."
